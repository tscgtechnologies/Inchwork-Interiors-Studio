Our Interior Design Process

We work in 5 clear, structured steps to ensure your home interiors are finalized exactly as planned, on time and within budget.

01

Requirement Discussion

Understand the client's home layout, design expectations, lifestyle needs, and budget goals.

02

Space Planning

Plan furniture placement, storage layouts, wardrobes, kitchen modules, and walk pathways.

03

Design Concept

Create visual concepts including finishes, laminates, false ceilings, lighting layouts, and colors.

04

Material & Budget Planning

Select laminate options, plywood grades, hardware accessories, and lock in the final quote.

05

Final Work & Handover

Execute precision carpentry and styling detailing, followed by final deep clean and handover.
